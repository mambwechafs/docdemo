Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about our healthcare services. If you have any other inquiries, feel free to contact us.

  • What healthcare services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of healthcare services including preventive care, diagnostics, treatments, surgeries, and specialized medical consultations.

  • How can I schedule an appointment?

    You can schedule an appointment by calling our office, visiting our website, or using our mobile app. Our friendly staff will assist you in finding a convenient time for your visit.

  • Do you accept insurance plans?

    Yes, we accept most major insurance plans including NHIMA. Please contact our billing department for more information about insurance coverage and payment options.

  • What should I bring to my appointment?

    Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results to your appointment.

  • How do I request a prescription refill?

    You can request a prescription refill by calling our office or using our online patient portal. Please allow 24-48 hours for prescription refills to be processed.
